About me

  • Trang Nguyen

    is my name and I stand behind midcut.

    I'm a Berlin-based filmmaker with Vietnamese origin. Being the first audience of the intended film, I see it in enormous amount of raw materials, and I compose it to what you see.

  • Work Style

    I'm passionated about storytelling techniques, forms and means. My motto: Dramatize life making documentary and make it real in fiction.

    Non-conventional forms of films, such as photofilm and installation interest me the most.

  • Background

    HFF “Konrad Wolf” is where I learned the art of film editing. When not making films, you would find me coding, cooking, knitting, biking or hiking.
