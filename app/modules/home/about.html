About me

Trang Nguyen is my name and Midcut is my film portfolio. I make documentary, fiction as well as installation. As a film editor, I take in enormous amounts of raw materials and I compose it into what you see. As a film director, I tell stories with the mean of images and sound. As an installation artist, I encourage you to take part in the story.

I’m passionated about storytelling techniques, forms and means. My motto: dramatize life making documentary and make it real in fiction.

My introduction to filmmaking was postproduction translation work where I saw the magic of composing images and sound. After editing two award winning films in Vietnam, I moved to Germany to pursuit the art of editing at the HFF “Konrad Wolf” filmschool in Potsdam.

During my undergrad study, I became interested in non-conventional forms of films, such as photofilm and installation, where an active and interactive participation of the audience is encouraged.

I spent my post-grad years strengthened my focus in photofilm and installation, giving hands-on photofilm-making course at the HFF. I live and work in Berlin. When not making films, you would find me coding, cooking, knitting, biking or hiking.
