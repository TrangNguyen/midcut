About me

Trang Nguyen is my name and Midcut is my film portfolio for my documentary, fiction as well as installation work. As an editor, I meticulously pick the best bits of footage to bring sight and sound to the director’s vision. As a director, I share with you my stories. As an installation artist, I encourage you to take part in the story.

I’m passionate about storytelling techniques, forms and means. My motto: dramatize life by making documentary films and make it real in fiction.

My introduction to filmmaking was postproduction translation work, where I saw the magic of binding images and sound. After editing two award winning films in Vietnam, I moved to Germany to pursue the art of editing at the HFF “Konrad Wolf” film school in Potsdam.

During my undergrad study, I became interested in non-conventional forms of films, such as photofilm and installation, where an active and interactive participation of the audience is encouraged. I spent my post-grad years strengthening my focus in photofilm and installation, giving introductory photofilm course at the HFF while editing my third full-length feature.

My goal is to continue working on interesting projects with fun, talented filmmakers. I'm based in Berlin, when not making films, you would find me coding, cooking, knitting, biking or hiking.
